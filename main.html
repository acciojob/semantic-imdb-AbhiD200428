The Plot

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief, the absolute best in the dangerous art of extraction, stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb’s rare ability has made him a coveted player in this treacherous new world of corporate espionage, but it has also made him an international fugitive. Now, he is offered a chance at redemption: Inception. Instead of the perfect heist, Cobb and his team of specialists have to pull off the reverse: the task is not to steal an idea, but to plant one.
